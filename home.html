Frequently Asked Questions

What is BrightFuture?

BrightFuture Books is all about discovery. We’ve applied over twenty years of experience as a customer-focused, online retailer to build a store that integrates the benefits of offline and online shopping and spurs discovery of great books, BrightFuture BrightFuturedevices and customer favorites from BrightFuture.com.

What can you buy at BrightFuture?

We carry a highly-curated selection of books, devices, toys and games that our customers love. All of our products are rated 4 stars and above by our customers, or are bestsellers, or new releases.

What kind of things are not Bright Future?

Currently, you won’t find any food! But, customers can visit any of our stores or one of the many Whole Foods Markets across the country.

What makes BrightFuture unique

BrightFuture Books is a customer-focused store, designed to spur discovery; a place where customers can find great books and products, and learn more about our devices. To give you more information as you browse, we’ve created unique features to highlight what people are reading, how they are reading and what they are loving, to help customers discover great new books

How do I pay for items? What’s checkout experience?

After you discover products you love, you visit our checkout registers toward the front of the store. We accept a variety of payment methods, including Amazon Pay via our mobile shopping app, Amazon gift cards, BrightFuture Cash, as well as Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

Who selects the books in the store?

We have a team of curators that study the information behind what people are reading, how they are reading and what they are loving, to bring forward a highly-curated selection of books that customers will love. They use BrightFuture.com customer ratings, reviews, pre-orders, sales, popularity on Goodreads, Kindle reading behavior and their own expertise to inform their selections. As an extension of Amazon.com, we have incorporated information from the millions of readers from Amazon.com into our stores to help Amazon Books customers discover books that are loved by fellow readers across the country.

How do you select non-books products for the store?

All of the books, devices, toys, games and other products in our stores are selected by our team of curators and are either rated 4 stars and above, or are a bestseller, or newly released. Devices from Amazon’s Echo, Kindle, Fire Tablet, and Fire TV families are also displayed and available for customers to test drive. Using this curated approach, we’re able to fill our store with the hottest releases, latest devices and hard to find toys that we know our customers will love.
